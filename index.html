Photograph Storage and Sharing

With Pic Metric, you can upload your pictures to the cloud and share them with your friends and family.

Element Identification

Pic Metric uses data science to identify the elements of a picture. Our dataset supports broad sets of categoiries ranging from people, plants and animals to various kinds of inanimate objects.

Meta data

Pic Metric allows users to upload and extract meta data from picture files. Meta data can have a lot of useful information, such as copyright, camera and equipment information, GPS tags, photograph manipulation tools used and much more.

About Pic Metric

Photograph Analysis

Pic Metric uses machine learning techniques to analyze your pictures and give you a breakdown of what you're photographing by category (people, places, things, etc). Pictures can be uploaded directly to the app or you can enter the url of a picture from another app or site.
